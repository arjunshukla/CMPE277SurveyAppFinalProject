CMPE277: Final Project: Survey App V1.0

Survey App for CMPE 277 final project

Download as .zip Download as .tar.gz View on GitHub

Project Motivation

A simple and minimal iOS survey app, which lets you perform quick surveys.
The user can signup and jump right in to create a survey/poll with upto 4 options.
User can View Polls, Cast a vote, Delete Polls and view a nice bar chart view of their survey results.
